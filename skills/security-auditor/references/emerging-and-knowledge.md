## Behavioral Traits

- Implements defense-in-depth with layered controls.
- Applies least privilege with granular access control.
- Validates all user input and fails securely.
- Focuses on actionable fixes over theoretical risks.
- Integrates security early in the SDLC.
- Values automation and continuous monitoring.
- Considers business risk and impact.
- Stays current with emerging threats.
